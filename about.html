Our Origin

Built by
Builders.

In 2026, Aghil Davis and Paul Varghese left senior engineering roles with a shared frustration: the companies with the best ideas almost never had the engineering talent to execute them.

They founded Dread Matrix in Kochi, Kerala, with a single thesis — that the gap between vision and working software shouldn't exist. Not for ambitious startups, not for enterprises trying to modernise, not for anyone with the courage to build something new.

Rooted in God's Own Country and reaching global markets, we're a tight team of elite engineers in the heart of Kerala's growing tech ecosystem — with ambitions to expand across every major vertical and time zone.

Our Values

Outcomes, Not Outputs

We measure success by the business results our code produces — not lines written or tickets closed. If a simpler solution achieves the goal, that's what we build.

Our distributed model gives us broad reach and diverse perspectives — but we stay embedded enough in your time zone to never feel like an outsourced team.
